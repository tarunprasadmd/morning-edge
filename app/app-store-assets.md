## Full Description (4000 char max)
Start each investing day grounded.

Morning Edge is your daily companion for disciplined investing — AI-generated market briefs paired with mindful check-ins. No accounts. No tracking. Just clarity.

WHAT YOU GET EVERY MORNING
- Personalized AI market briefs based on your portfolio
- Fidelity CSV upload — your holdings stay on your device
- Three pillars: Wealth, Health, Clarity — investing as a whole-person practice
- A focused morning ritual, not market noise

WHO THIS IS FOR
For investors who want their day to start with clarity, not chaos. Built for people who believe discipline beats reactivity, and that mornings set the tone for everything that follows.

WHAT MAKES MORNING EDGE DIFFERENT
- Built by a 20-year senior clinical healthcare professional, not a finance industry insider
- Privacy-first: portfolio data processed locally, never sent to our servers
- No social feed, no notifications, no FOMO — just a focused morning brief
- Honest disclaimers: this is informational, not advice

OUR MISSION
A portion of proceeds supports U.S. charities helping children and adults with disabilities, and the families who care for them. Every install carries that purpose forward.

IMPORTANT
Morning Edge is for informational and educational use only. Not investment, financial, tax, or medical advice. Past performance does not guarantee future results. AI-generated content may be inaccurate — verify before acting. Consult a licensed financial advisor or healthcare professional before making decisions.
